Who is KJ Apa? – Biography and Age

Even though it is simply as KJ Apa that the actor is very popular, he was born Keneti James Fitzgerald Apa on June 17, 1997 in his home country of New Zealand, Auckland. Her father, Keneti Apa, is a traditional Samoan chief and her mother, Tessa Apa, is a European New Zealander. He was raised alongside two older sisters.

Before starting his acting career, he went to King’s College in Auckland where he was educated. He grew up loving the guitar, which he still plays in his spare time, although he has no interest in pursuing a musical career yet. Also, he was a sports athlete who played rugby.

Another thing he loves is shooting. In 2017, he was involved in a minor car accident which allegedly took place while he had fallen asleep after being shot. Former rugby union player and coach, Michael Jones is his uncle.

KJ Apa’s first acting role came with an appearance on Shortland Street in 2013 as Kane Jenkins. After that, he got the role of Jack in 2016 ‘s Dead End, and then his real big break came when he got his role in 2017’s Riverdale as Archie Andrews. Apart from the series, he has also appeared in several films such as A Dog’s Purpose in 2017, Altar, Hatred U Give in 2018 and The Last Summer in 2019.

The talented AJ Apa received several nominations for his role in Riverdale such as the Teen Choice Awards and the MTV Movie & TV Awards. He received the Saturn Awards for Outstanding Performance in 2017 for the same role.

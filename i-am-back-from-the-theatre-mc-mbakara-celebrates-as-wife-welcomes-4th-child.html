MC Mbakara, a Nigerian content creator, has welcomed his fourth child with his darling wife, Rajunor Aya, also known as Lolo.

Mbakara took to social media to share his joy with his fans and friends. He revealed that he was back from the theatre as he shared an update from the hospital. He also thanked everyone for their kind words and prayers.

MC Mbakara and wife welcome child

In a now-trending video, Mbakara, who is known for creating content with his wife, was seen carrying his newborn child out of the theatre.

Biography

Mohammed Kudus (born August 2, 2000) is a highly experienced professional footballer. 

He showcases his talent as an attacking midfielder or winger for the renowned Premier League club West Ham United and represents the Ghana national team. Before this, Mohammed Kudus honed his abilities while playing for Nordsjælland and Ajax.

Early Life & Education

Mohammed Kudus, a talented football player, was born on August 2, 2000, in the vibrant city of Accra in Ghana. Growing up in the lively suburb of Nima, he had the privilege of being surrounded by a close-knit community. 

Raised by his loving mother, Mariama Suleman, Mohammed Kudus‘s childhood was filled with love and support. His father, Suleman Idris Kudus, is a businessman. Speedster is a brother to him.

However, this setback did not deter Mohammed from pursuing his dreams. With two siblings by his side, Mohammed Kudus attended a public school in Nima, where he received his education while nurturing his passion for football.

Career

Mohammed Kudus embarked on his football journey with Ghana’s Right to Dream Academy, and in 2018, he joined Nordsjælland, a Danish club. He showcased his talents with ten goals in 40 matches across various competitions during his time there. His abilities didn’t go unnoticed, leading to a €9 million transfer to Ajax in July 2020.

Mohammed Kudus made an immediate impact at Ajax, scoring three goals and providing five assists in his first ten matches starting in September 2020. Unfortunately, a severe knee injury in December of the same year temporarily halted his progress.

But Mohammed Kudus bounced back in April 2021 and played a crucial role in Ajax’s Eredivisie title victory. He also shone on the Champions League stage, featuring in ten matches, including two starts during the semi-finals against Tottenham Hotspur.

In the 2021–22 season, Mohammed Kudus continued to impress, notching four goals and six assists in all competitions. Additionally, he made his debut for the Ghana national team in November 2021.

Transfer News

In the summer transfer window of August 2023, a significant move occurred as Mohammed Kudus made his way to West Ham United in a highly anticipated transfer deal worth an impressive €43 million. 

With this substantial investment, the club has high hopes for the talented player and expects him to play a crucial role in the Premier League and the Europa League competitions.

Can I get free shipping at Adam & Eve?

Yes, you can currently enjoy free shipping on all orders over $59 which will take 5-8 working days. Simply enter the Adam & Eve free shipping code that you can find on their website at the checkout stage. 

Does Adam & Eve offer a new customer discount?

In the past, Adam & Eve have offered new customer discounts of up to 50% off your first order when you sign up to their newsletter. During times when this promotion is unavailable, you can use one of our Adam & Eve promo codes to help you save. 

Can I get a special offer at Adam & Eve?

Adam & Eve occasionally have special offers like free gifts when you shop available on their website. It’s a good idea to keep an eye out for these are they are typically time-limited promotions. 

What is the returns policy at Adam & Eve?

At Adam & Eve, they have a 90-day returns policy. The cost of making a return is the customer’s responsibility. To start your returns process, fill out the returns form on the Adam & Eve website.

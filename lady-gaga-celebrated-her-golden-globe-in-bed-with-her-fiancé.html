Lady Gaga Celebrated Her Golden Globe in Bed With Her Fianc

 Posted on September 5, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  204 words  |  Larita Shotwell

After the party is ... bed, apparently. Lady Gaga, a woman after our own hearts, certainly knows this to be true.

At the 2019 Golden Globes, Gaga looked ready to take the show by storm, outfitted in a periwinkle Valentino gown, which is believed to be an homage to Judy Garland's dress from when she played the same role in the 1954 version of A Star Is Born. But just a few hours later, Gaga looked ready for bed.

The singer's fiancé, talent agent Christian Carino, posted a shot of Gaga post-Globes looking cozy in bed with all of the essentials. And by essentials, we mean her trophy and Fruity Pebbles, because apparently fame has not altered her impeccable taste in midnight snacks.

Carino captioned the black-and-white photo, "what a rager," with a single red heart.

Gaga shed a proud tear or two as she took home the Best Original Song award for the track "Shallow," however she lost the Globe for Best Actress in a Motion Picture – Drama to Glenn Close. The singer and Close shared a touching moment nonetheless, holding hands and exchanging congratulations as Close ascended the stage.

All in all, a great night for Gaga.

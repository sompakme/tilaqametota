Brandi Rhodes Makes Shocking Revelation About Her Wrestling Run

 Posted on August 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  265 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Brandi Rhodes came from the unknown and made her name in the wrestling world. Moreover, she even got married into wrestling royalty. However, Brandi recently opened up about a shocking fact about her tenure in the business.

She began her wrestling career as a WWE ring announcer, Eden Stiles gaining prominence. However, Brandi exited the company in 2016 right after the departure of her real-life husband, The American Nightmare Cody Rhodes.

The duo traveled across the world, wrestling for various promotions including WWE’s top rival, AEW. Moreover, they even held managerial roles in Tony Khan’s company. But Cody and Brandi Rhodes left the promotion in 2022.

While her days in the wrestling business might be over for now, Brandi Rhodes recently revealed on X that a bunch of people during that time gave her a hard time. But she also mentioned that those people have paid the price for making Brandi’s time hard and she does not seek any pleasure from it.

“There were a handful of legit idiots who made my time in wrestling harder than it needed to be. But when I hear of the cold dishes of karma they’ve been served, it gives me absolutely no pleasure. (This was me giving my daughter an example of what a bold faced lie is).”

Brandi Rhodes has dedicated herself to her child and other outdoor assignments, while her husband, Cody Rhodes is eyeing to finish his story in the main event of WrestleMania 40 this year.

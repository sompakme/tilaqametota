Iyabo Ojo biography

The Nigerian actress was born on December 21st, 1977. Her parents are originally from Abeokuta in Ogun State, but she was born and raised in Lagos State, which is located in the southwest of Nigeria. She is the last born in a family of three. She has two elder brothers.

How old is Iyabo Ojo?

Having been born in 1977, Iyabo Ojo age is 43 years as of 2021. She celebrates her birthday on the 21st day of December each year.

Educational background

The famous actress embodies beauty and braina. She attended school in Lagos at National College, Gbagbada. After graduating, she joined Lagos State Polytechnic to pursue a Higher National Diploma (HND) in Estate Management.

Her illustrious career

Iyabo Ojo is one of the key figures in Nigeria's film industry. She has acted in various top-selling movies.

She has also produced and directed several Yoruba and Nollywood movies. Below are her career highlights.

As an actress

Iyabo's passion for acting was evident during her time in secondary school. She was a member of the school's drama group, and her active involvement sharpened her acting skills.

She made her acting debut in the Nigerian film industry in 1998. She was part of the cast for Satanic. Her impressive role in this particular movie catapulted her to the limelight and she never looked back.

The actress is passionate about empowering people through her work. She founded a Non-Governmental Organisation (NGO) called Pinkies Foundation.

The NGO was launched in 2011, and its objective is to help less privileged children, especially those living with disabilities. In 2015, she was part of the movie titled Beyond Disability - a touching and educative film.

Awards and achievements

The actress' impressive contribution to the success of most Nollywood and Yoruba films has not been in vain. She has received awards and recognition from various accredited bodies.

Some of her notable awards include:

  • In 2007, in the United Kingdom, she won the Best Crossover Actress during African Film Awards.
  • In 2011, she won the Excellent Performance on Stage during the BOTRA Award.
  • In 2011, she was awarded the Best Feature Film at the Abuja International Film Festival.
  • In 2011, she won the Award of Excellence and Recognition by the Deluxe Magazine.
  • In 2012, she emerged the Best Actress in a Supporting Role at the Best of Nollywood Awards
  • In 2013, Iyabo won the Best Actress of the Year during the CAMA Awards.
  • In 2015, she was named the Woman of the Year at Africa Mouthpiece.
  • In 2015, she was named the Best Actress of the Year (Yoruba) at the City People Movie Award.

Is Iyabo Ojo married?

The actress is currently not married. However, she was married before, but her marriage was not meant to last a lifetime.

She got married in 1999. Not long after, she gave birth to her first child named Festus. In 2001, she brought another child into the world, and it was a girl. Iyabo Ojo daughter is called her Priscillah.

Not much about Iyabo Ojo husband is known except that he is a Lagos-based movie marketer named Ojo. A while back, it was rumoured that he was the one who helped her to become an instant hit in Nollywood.

The award-winning actress refuted this allegation. She further divulged that the reason why he married her was that she was pregnant. She said,

My husband married me because I was pregnant for him; that was the main reason. Most men get married to ladies in the industry because of the celebrity aura around them, only to realise that there is a lot more to marriage than that.

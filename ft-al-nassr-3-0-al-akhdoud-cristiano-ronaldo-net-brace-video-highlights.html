FT: Al Nassr 3-0 Al Akhdoud, Cristiano Ronaldo Net BRACE! (VIDEO HIGHLIGHTS)

 Posted on August 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  322 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Cristiano Ronaldo with another outstanding performance as Al Nassr claimed a 3-0 win against Al Akhdoud, MySportDab reports.

Cristiano Ronaldo brace was enough to earn his side the needed victory which pushed them close to overtaking the number one team.

The game was an easy victory for the home side who narrow down points between them and Al Hilal who will play their match tomorrow.

Full Match Report

Incredible scene as both teams came into the match with a lot of objective and quest to make sure they took all the maximum points.

Al Akhdoud gave a good account of themselves starting well as they held on for the first 10 minutes making sure nothing hit them.

Al Nassr were able to take the lead in the 13th minutes with some beautiful play which saw Sami Al Naji ensuring things between them became 1-0.

There was a lot which happened in the remaining minutes of the half which was more of opportunities but none could change what was on ground.

Al Akhdoud were unable to pull any string as Al Nassr finished the first half with a 1-0 lead against their opponent who gave a good fight.

There was more to play in the remaining minutes of the game which presented more opportunities and goals registered.

It wasn’t pleasant at any moment for Al Akhdoud who did more defending and couldn’t push much with attacking knowing it was tight.

Al Nassr increased their lead with Cristiano Ronaldo in the 77th minutes from an incredible finish which ensured things were 2-0.

Ronaldo was called into action again making it a brace in the 80th minutes which was yet again a clever finish making it a 3-0 scoreline as they continued.

Al Nassr claimed needed three points moving closer to Al Hilal who top with one after being able to dispatch Al Akhdoud 3-0 after 90 minutes.

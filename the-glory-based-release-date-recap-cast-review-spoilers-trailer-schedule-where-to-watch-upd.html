What are the spoilers for The Glory Based?

The Glory Based is a revenge drama that revolves around Moon Dong-eun, a woman who was severely bullied in high school by a group of five students: Park Yeon-jin, Jeon Jae-joon, Lee Sa-ra, Choi Hye-jeong, and Son Myeong-oh. The bullying was so extreme that Dong-eun suffered physical and psychological scars, and she had to drop out of school. She also lost her family, who were bribed by Yeon-jin’s parents to abandon her.

Dong-eun vowed to get revenge on her bullies, and she spent years studying and saving money to execute her plan. She became a teacher at the elementary school where Yeon-jin’s daughter, Ye-sol, was studying. She also hired a private investigator, Ha Do-yeong, to gather information on her targets. She then started to manipulate and expose them one by one, using their weaknesses and secrets against them.

Storyline & Plot Summary

Yeon-jin was the leader of the bullies and the most vicious one. She married a wealthy businessman, Joo Yeo-jeong, and became a socialite. She also had a secret affair with Jae-joon, who was the owner of a fashion store and Myeong-oh’s boss. Sa-ra was an artist and a drug addict, who was blackmailed by Dong-eun to sabotage Yeon-jin’s art exhibition. Hye-jeong was a flight attendant, who was framed by Dong-eun for smuggling drugs and arrested at the airport. Myeong-oh was a thug, who was lured by Dong-eun to a warehouse and beaten up by her hired men.

Dong-eun’s final target was Yeon-jin, who she planned to ruin by exposing her affair, her involvement in the bullying, and her attempt to kill Dong-eun. She also wanted to take away her daughter, Ye-sol, who she had befriended and influenced. However, things got complicated when Yeo-jeong, Yeon-jin’s husband, turned out to be Dong-eun’s childhood friend and crush, who had moved away before the bullying started. Yeo-jeong recognized Dong-eun and tried to help her, but he also wanted to protect his family.

  • Episode 3: The Betrayal. Dong-eun lures Myeong-oh to a warehouse and has him beaten up by her hired men. She also reveals to him that Jae-joon is having an affair with Yeon-jin. Yeo-jeong visits Dong-eun at her home and sees her scars from the bullying.
  • Episode 4: The Confession. Dong-eun confronts Jae-joon at his store and threatens to expose his affair. She also tells him that she knows he was one of her bullies.   • Episode 5: The Secret. Dong-eun finds out that Yeon-jin tried to kill her by pushing her off a bridge when they were in high school. She also learns that Yeon-jin’s parents bribed her parents to abandon her. Yeo-jeong invites Dong-eun to his home and introduces her to Ye-sol.
  • Episode 6: The Reunion. Dong-eun meets Yeon-jin at a charity event and pretends to be friendly with her. She also meets the other bullies, who do not recognize her. Yeo-jeong tells Dong-eun that he loves her and wants to be with her. Dong-eun tells him that she loves him too, but she is lying.

  • Episode 8: The Cliffhanger. Dong-eun is kidnapped by Do-yeong, who takes her to a remote cabin. He tells her that he loves her and wants to run away with her. Dong-eun tries to escape, but Do-yeong shoots her in the chest. The episode ends with Dong-eun lying on the floor, bleeding.

Dua Lipa Looks Fabulous in a See-Through Lace Jumpsuit and Black Lingerie

 Posted on August 25, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  325 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Dua Lipa has officially touched down in Italy.

Today, the Grammy-winning singer dropped jaws when she arrived at the GCDS runway show during Milan Fashion Week, where she wore a sexy lace number. Her see-through black mesh jumpsuit included a mock turtleneck, a cutout on the backside, and long sleeves that extended into gloves. Underneath, she wore a black bra and underwear, as well as a pair of black platform heels. A bright red heart-shaped purse and a matching mani capped off the scene-stealing look.

As for glam, Lipa wore subtle shimmering gray eye shadow and a light brown lip; her sweeping jet black hair was worn down and straightened, framing her face.

Daniele Venturelli//Getty Images

Daniele Venturelli//Getty Images

Earlier in the day, the "New Rules" singer sat front row at Prada's fall/winter 2023 fashion show. Then, she layered a boxy black blazer over a tan-and-pink collared shirt emblazoned with a brown triangle pattern. In addition to wearing sheer black tights and chunky black loafers, she accessorized with a small black purse, Prada triangle logo earrings, and an array of sparkling rings.

Jacopo M. Raule//Getty Images

Lipa previously opened up about her experimental and playful style in a 2018 interview with Refinery29.

"I've never really wanted to put myself in a box and say, 'This is my style and these are my style staples.' I love to experiment and play around," she told the outlet. "It's kind of like music: The sound doesn't just include the face of the artist. And the artist should wear the clothes, not the other way around. Fashion can't be forced."

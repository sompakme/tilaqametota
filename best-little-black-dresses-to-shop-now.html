Miscreants

We all know the power a simple black dress can have. But why not have some fun with it? Miscreants strikes the perfect balance between statement-making and wearability. Its classic black dresses are contrasted with OTT puff-sleeved gloves and bright coloured bows – it's dressing-up with an elegant yet extravagant touch.  

Credit: Courtesy

When it comes to our wardrobes, investing in key staple styles to wear and love forever is one of the greenest ways to shop. Gone are the days where come party season, we want a whole new wardrobe. Instead, it's all about choosing brands that are built to last and partying the night away in them for seasons to come. This number by Galvan will see you through year after year.

7

Clio Peppiatt

Credit: Courtesy

Clio Peppiatt's mini dresses are instantly recognisable and something just so special. Each dress is like a work of art, adorned with shimmering beads and artwork you'd see in a museum. Inspired by the Medieval French ‘Apocalypse Tapestries’ commissioned by Louis I, this dramatic piece showcases dragons and avenging angels.

8

Molly Goddard

Credit: Courtesy

Pay homage to one of the most iconic partywear seasons, the Eighties. Molly Goddard's mini dress is fit for the occasion, featuring a one-shoulder neckline and a slender bodice structured from bows and ruching. Team with polka-dot tights and heels for a retro look, not forgetting the bright eyeshadow fit for the era.

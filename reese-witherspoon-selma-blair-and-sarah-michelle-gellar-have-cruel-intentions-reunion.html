Reese Witherspoon, Selma Blair and Sarah Michelle Gellar have Cruel Intentions reunion

 Posted on July 15, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  372 words  |  Larita Shotwell

    It was, in Reese Witherspoon's own words, the "best girls night of the year" – and fans of the 1999 hit film would surely agree. On Thursday evening, Reese reunited with the movie co-stars Selma Blair and Sarah Michelle Gellar for a fun evening out together in LA.The trio found the perfect setting for their get-together. They headed out to watch the unauthorised musical parody of at Rockwell Table & Stage in Los Feliz.

Just a few days later, Ryan – Reese's ex-husband - went along to watch the parody and posed with cast members backstage.While Ryan was absent from this week's reunion, all three ladies took to social media to share their excitement about the girls night. Coincidentally, Thursday also marked the 49th birthday of director Roger Krumble, and Selma also shared a throwback photo of herself with "the man who made that movie, one of my favourite people".

FAQs

Does Shopbop offer free shipping?

Yes, Shopbop has a free 1-3 day shipping option for all orders in the US. Shopbop is also affiliated with Amazon Prime, meaning you can get free 2-day shipping and free Saturday delivery with eligible Amazon products. 

How often does Shopbop have sales?

You can shop the Shopbop sale all year round and find discounts of up to 60% off. Shopbop also features sales throughout the year, often around holidays and as part of special events. If you missed the latest Shopbop promo, save on your next order with a Shopbop coupon. 

Does Shopbop have a welcome offer?

Yes, new customers can receive a 15% Shopbop discount code to use on their first order when they sign up to receive emails from Shopbop. Of course, you'll make some great savings, but you'll also be the first to know about the latest sale events and exclusive offers from Shopbop. 

Is there a Shopbop price matching guarantee?

There is both a price match guarantee and a price adjustment available at Shopbop. If you find the same product for less at another store, you can get in touch with their Customer Service team and they will match the price. Equally, if your product is discounted within 7 days of your purchase date, you can request a price adjustment too. 

Does Shopbop offer free returns?

Yes, Shopbop has a free 15-day return policy. The 15-day countdown starts as soon as the item is marked as delivered by the carrier. Items that are labeled as final sale are not eligible for the 15-day return policy. If you return your purchase within 16-30 days, returns will cost $10.

Newsletter Offers: Stay up to date with all things Shopbop when you sign up to their newsletter using your email. You'll be notified of upcoming sales, receive exclusive offers and Shopbop coupons, and more, making it a great option for regular customers looking to save money in the long run.

Shopbop App: Download the Shopbop app to unlock 15% off your first order. You'll also enjoy a stress-free online shopping experience where you can shop for your Shopbop favorites and stay up to date with the latest discounts, all from your couch. 

How to use a Shopbop coupon

  • Find your ideal Shopbop coupon on the Marie Claire voucher site and click 'Get Code' to copy it. 
  • Head over to the Shopbop website and begin your shop. 
  • Add the items you want to purchase to your cart. On Shopbop, this is referred to as a shopping bag.
  • Open the shopping bag, or cart, to get to the checkout screen.
  • On the right-hand side of the checkout screen, look for a section titled “Promo Code”.
  • Enter your chosen Shopbop coupon code in the empty field.
  • Click Apply.
  • If your Shopbop code is valid, it will be applied to your purchase. You can now complete the checkout.

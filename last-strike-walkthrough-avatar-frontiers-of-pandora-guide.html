Last Strike Walkthrough - Avatar: Frontiers of Pandora Guide

 Posted on September 28, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  366 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Equip your SID tool and scan the panel in front of the big window. You must solve a security-level 5 hack puzzle in order to raise the drill charge. You only have forty-five seconds to solve it, on top of whatever time you have remaining before the drill charge detonates. Solve the puzzle before time runs out.

Get to Teylan

Run to the door to the left labeled "Corridor B". The floor is electrified, and you need to shoot the red power box to the left inside the door to shut off the electricity. At the top of the slope, the floor is electrified again, and you must shoot the red power box to the left to get through.

Punch the vent on the other side to exit the shaft. In the corridor beyond, a piece of electrified rubble hangs from the ceiling. Shoot the red power box on this as well to pass under the obstacle safely.

Move towards the end of the corridor and punch the vent at the end to get inside. Shoot the red box in this shaft as well to pass through without the ground electrifying you. Then shoot the next vent cover to reveal a red power box behind it. Shoot it from a distance to turn off the electricity. Inside this vent, punch the grate in the floor repeatedly to escape.

You will drop down into a new room, triggering a series of cutscenes. This marks the end of the Last Strike quest and completes all main quests for Avatar: Frontiers of Pandora!

Can You Play Avatar: Frontiers of Pandora After Beating the Game?

Yes! Once you have completed the Last Strike quest and let the credits roll, you will be dropped back at the Resistance Hideout, essentially right where you were before you triggered the final quest. You will unlock some rewards for beating the main quests, including the Exquisite So'lek Merciless Longbow.

While there is no "post-game story" per se, once you have beaten the main quests, you can continue playing Avatar: Frontiers of Pandora just as you were before you started the last quest.

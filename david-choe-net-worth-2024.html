David Choe’s Early Life and Career

David Choe was born in Los Angeles, California, to Korean immigrant parents. He developed an interest in art at a young age and pursued his passion despite facing numerous challenges. Choe’s career took off when he started painting murals and creating graphic novels. His work quickly gained recognition for its raw and provocative style.

Breakthrough with Facebook Murals

Choe’s breakthrough came when he was commissioned to paint murals for Facebook’s first headquarters in 2005. Instead of cash payment, Choe chose to be compensated in Facebook stock, a decision that would later make him a multimillionaire when the company went public in 2012.

David Choe’s Artistic Style and Influence

Choe’s art is known for its chaotic and vibrant style, often featuring distorted figures and intense, contrasting colors. His work is heavily influenced by his experiences and personal struggles, making it deeply emotional and relatable to many.

Recognition and Exhibitions

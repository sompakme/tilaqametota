The Miz Boasts About Celebrity Wheel Of Fortune Appearance

 Posted on July 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  231 words  |  Larita Shotwell

The Miz has been off the WWE shows since while now. While he was supposed to feud against his former tag team partner, John Morrison, it seems he has different plans about his time now that they’re off-screen.

The Awesome WWE superstar is set to appear on a couple of shows, such as a show on Nickelodeon. He has also been bagged for Dancing With The Stars season 30, which is a huge opportunity for the WWE star.

The Miz has scored himself another appearance, and it’s something that many of his fellow WWE superstars are hyped about. The Miz will be appearing on Celebrity Wheel of Fortune alongside Cheryl Burke & Seinfeld’s Jason Alexander. The Miz took to Twitter in order to shot out a tweet regarding his appearance. He captioned it:

_ H M Y G _ D!

I’d like to buy an O please because this Sunday I’m going to check another item off my bucket list when I’m a contestant on the Season Premiere of Celebrity @WheelofFortune. Now that’s Awesome!!!!

The New Day’s Xavier Woods also gave him shout out, while tweeting “I’ve never been more jealous of you in my life.” The Miz recently crossed a career-defining landmark at WWE, as he completed 15 years since his main roster debut.

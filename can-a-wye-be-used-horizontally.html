Can a wye then be installed horizontally?

A wye can be laid on its side as long as it provides the proper grade or slope – 97 UPC 708, 97 IPC table 704.1. 1997 UPC section 706.3 states specifically that horizontal drainage lines connecting with other horizontal drainage lines must enter through 45 degree wye branches.

Is it also possible to run sewer vent horizontally?

 To answer your specific question, vent pipes can have horizontal runs as long as they do not become clogged with water. In other words, any water that enters the vent pipe opening must be free to flow all the way to the sewer without forming a “trap.”

In this case, can a sanitary tee be used horizontally?

When connecting a horizontal run, such as a waste arm, to a vent stack or vertical riser, a sanitary tee should be used.

In a PVC DWV system, a combo wye (HxHxH) is used to connect a horizontal branch line to a vertical drain pipe.

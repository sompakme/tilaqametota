Do autoimmune diseases make you get sick easier?

 Posted on October 1, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1244 words  |  Larita Shotwell
If you have an autoimmune disease, your immune system attacks healthy cells in your body by mistake. Other immune system problems happen when your immune system does not work correctly. These problems include immunodeficiency diseases. If you have an immunodeficiency disease, you get sick more often.

Is it easier to get sick if you have an autoimmune disease?

People with autoimmune disorders have been described as the population at the most risk of catching diseases. This is due to the way the different autoimmune disorders affect their immune system, and more importantly, to the immunosuppressant drugs used to treat most of these diseases.

Is someone with an autoimmune disease more susceptible to Covid?

If you have an autoimmune disorder, you are not more likely to get COVID-19. However, depending on the autoimmune disorder and the immunosuppressive medication you are taking, you may be more likely to get seriously ill from COVID-19.

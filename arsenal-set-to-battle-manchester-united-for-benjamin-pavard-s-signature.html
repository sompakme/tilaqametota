Arsenal set to battle Manchester United for Benjamin Pavard's signature

 Posted on September 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  391 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

- The Gunners have shown interest in signing France international Benjamin Pavard this summer

- He is also wanted by Manchester United and Bayern Munich as VfB Stuttgart are open to selling him after the World Cup

- The 22-year-old scored a stunning side volley that helped his side beat Argentina in the knockout stage

Premier League giants Arsenal are reportedly planning to beat Manchester United to the signature of French star Benjamin Pavard.

The 22-year-old defender scored a stunning goal in their round of 16 clash against Argentina earlier this month as they beat the South American giants to reach the quarterfinal.

However, according to Tribal football, his German League club side VfB Stuttgart are ready to listen to offers from top European clubs after the World Cup.

The news outlet also revealed that the French star is also wanted by Bayern Munich as they continue to strengthen their squad for competition next term.

Meanwhile, the right back was quoted to have disclosed that he will leave his present club for a Champions League side before the coming season.

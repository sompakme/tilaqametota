Who was Sherika De Armas? All About Miss Uruguay 2015

 Posted on August 26, 2024  |  7 minutes  |  1284 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Sherika De Armas was a former Miss Uruguay and a model who represented her country at the Miss World 2015 pageant. She passed away on October 13, 2023, at the age of 26, after a two-year battle with cervical cancer. Her death has shocked and saddened many people who admired her beauty, talent, and courage.

Early Life and Education

Sherika De Armas was born in 1997 in Montevideo, Uruguay. She grew up in a loving family with her parents and two brothers. She attended the instituto de modelos Silvia Holly (Valentino Bookings), where she learned the skills and techniques of modeling. She also studied business administration at the Universidad de la República.

Modeling Career

Sherika Armas started her modeling career at a young age and worked with various agencies and brands. She participated in several fashion shows and events, both nationally and internationally. She was also featured in magazines and advertisements.

In 2015, she won the title of Miss Uruguay and earned the right to represent her country at the Miss World 2015 pageant in China. Although she did not make it to the top 30 finalists, she impressed the judges and the audience with her elegance, charisma, and intelligence. She also won the People’s Choice Award at the pageant.

Apart from modeling, Sherika De also had a passion for beauty and wellness. She sold hair, makeup, and personal care products through her own business, Shey De Armas Beauty Studio. She also had a large following on social media platforms such as Facebook and Instagram, where she shared her photos, videos, and tips with her fans.

Miss Uruguay 2015

De Armas was crowned Miss Uruguay 2015 on June 4, 2015, at the Radisson Victoria Plaza Hotel in Montevideo. She was the winner among 18 contestants who competed for the prestigious title. She received the crown from the outgoing Miss Uruguay 2014, Johana Riva.

As Miss Uruguay 2015, Sherika De Armas represented her country at the Miss World 2015 pageant in Sanya, China, on December 19, 2015. She was one of the 114 delegates who vied for the coveted crown of Miss World. She did not make it to the top 30 finalists, but she won the People’s Choice Award, which was based on online voting by fans around the world.

Sherika De Armas was the first Miss Uruguay to win the People’s Choice Award at Miss World. She also made history as the youngest Miss Uruguay ever, as she was only 18 years old when she competed at Miss World. She impressed the judges and the audience with her beauty, elegance, charisma, and intelligence. She also showcased her talent in singing and dancing during the pageant.

Sherika De Armas was proud of her achievement and expressed her gratitude to her supporters on social media. She wrote on her Facebook page: “Thank you very much to all who supported me in this beautiful experience that I will never forget. I am very happy and satisfied with everything I did and learned. I gave my best and I hope I have represented Uruguay well. I love you all!”

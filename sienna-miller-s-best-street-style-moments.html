Sienna Miller's best street style moments

 Posted on September 16, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  467 words  |  Martina Birk

The actress and eternal fashion muse has the wardrobe of our dreams…

    Fashion darling Sienna Miller captured hearts back in the mid-2000s with her boho-infused It-girl aesthetic. Denim waistcoats, cowboy boots, studded belts and swathes of suede, the noughties style icon was ahead of her time – especially now that boho is back with a bang thanks to Chemena Kamali's debut show for hippy-adjacent designer label Chloé. 

    But Sienna's style prowess goes way beyond her golden era, as evidenced by some of her more recent fashion triumphs.

    Hello! Fashion shares Sienna Miller's best street style moments:

    1/10

    Balloon Jeans

    At the Cannes photocall for , Sienna tapped into one of the hottest denim trends of the moment: balloon jeans. Expanding at the knee before tapering in at the ankle, the style is unexpectedly super flattering. Sienna paired her dark denim wash pair with a cream blazer with playful, surrealist gold motifs, courtesy of Schiaparelli. 

    2/10

    Hippyish Skirt

    The actress made her presence known at Paris Fashion Week in head-to-toe Chloé. She layered a whimsical white lace slip under a heavy-duty leather jacket and completed her ensemble with vertiginous wooden wedges.

    3/10

    Cool-Girl Skort

    The star went for simplicity, opting for a light collared knit, tinted shades and a black skort with a distinctly sporty feel.

    4/10

    Neutral Power Suit

    Sharp and elegant, Sienna wore a beige double-breasted suit to Wimbledon with acetate accessories and kitten-heel mules.

    5/10

    70s Glam

    The actress oozed sultry, 1970s spirit in a plunging camel and brown dress, styled alongside a mirrored clutch and blue suede platforms.

    6/10

    The Fresh White Midi

    Sienna served up pretty, summer elegance in a lace Bardot dress, styled with a black leather belt focal point for a bit of edge. Her snakeskin T-bar sandals provided a sense of texture, and she opted for a blurred red lip for a pop of colour. 

    7/10

    Sultry Lace Leggings

    Sienna exuded after-hours sultriness in an all-black ensemble. The actress styled a cut-out LBD over stirrup-style lace leggings alongside a formal overcoat and stilettos.

    8/10

    Pinstripe Co-ord

    The picture of easy-breezy summer cool, Sienna wore a blue and white striped shirt with matching wide-leg trousers and a cream top-handle bag.

    9/10

    Ruffle Knit

    The actress was a 1970s dream in a ruffle multi-coloured knit and high-wasted jeans paired with a monochrome structured shoulder bag.

    10/10

    Groundbreaking Florals

    The street style maven delivered a corker with this orange short-sleeved midaxi featuring a pretty daisy print. Sienna styled the piece with barely-there sandals and a gold clutch.

Drama Unleashed: Iyabo Ojo Hits Lizzy Anjorin with N500 Million Lawsuit

 Posted on September 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  284 words  |  Martina Birk

The ongoing Nollywood drama between actresses Iyabo Ojo and Lizzy Anjorin has escalated as Iyabo‘s legal team issues a letter demanding an apology and N500 million in damages over alleged libel.

The feud started when Lizzy Anjorin made various allegations against Iyabo Ojo, including claims that she was romantically involved with singer Naira Marley and accused her of being connected to the anonymous Instagram blog, Gistlover.

In response, Iyabo Ojo‘s lawyers have given Liz Anjorin 14 days to issue an apology and pay the substantial sum of N500 million for the alleged defamatory publications against the actress online.

The legal documents, shared by Iyabo Ojo on her social media, detail the demands and consequences if Lizzy fails to comply within the given timeframe. The actress wrote, “14 days is around the corner. Saga activated.”

This legal move comes after public accusations and counter-accusations between the two actresses. Lizzy Anjorin had conducted Instagram Live sessions making veiled references to Iyabo Ojo and her alleged involvement in the activities of the mysterious blog, Gistlover. The feud escalated with Lizzy connecting Iyabo to the death of street-pop singer Mohbad.

The lawsuit, initiated by Iyabo Ojo through her lawyer Olumide Alake, demands a public apology and a retraction of Lizzy Anjorin‘s alleged defamatory statements. The legal team warned of further legal action if Lizzy fails to respond within the stipulated 14-day period.

This legal showdown adds a new chapter to the ongoing saga, revealing the depth of the conflict between the two Nollywood actresses. The N500 million lawsuit has stirred anticipation among fans and followers as they watch the unfolding legal drama between Iyabo Ojo and Lizzy Anjorin.

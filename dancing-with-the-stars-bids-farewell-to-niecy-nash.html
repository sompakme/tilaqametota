Dancing with the Stars Bids Farewell to Niecy Nash

 Posted on September 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  230 words  |  Martina Birk

Somewhat predictably, Niecy Nash was eliminated from Dancing with the Stars on Tuesday after receiving the lowest scores from judges and viewers alike.

Voted off Dancing with the Stars after the eighth week, the How Clean is Your House host and actress on Reno 911 took the elimination well, and in stride.

In her exit interview, she says she was proud to make it this far as a 40-year-old mom of three against some of the great dancers remaining on the stage.

Tom, admitting he’s a fan, complimented her on achieving moments of elegance with partner Louis Van Amstel, of which there were surprisingly many.

Her last request? That the band play something so that everyone can “get krunk.” That’s why Niecy Nash is awesome, and how you go out in style.


Niecy Nash and Louis Van Amstel dance farewell.

Chad Ochocinco and Cheryl Burke joined Niecy and Louis in the bottom two for the first time, which was fitting, given the two couples’ lower Monday scores.

Whether he can skate by into the final three is another story.

Nicole Scherzinger and Derek Hough performed an encore of their perfect Paso Doble from the other night, which Tom deemed the best in the show’s history.

What are spaghetti hoops made of?

 Posted on July 10, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  949 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill
What are spaghetti hoops made of? Most brands use durum wheat semolina to make the pasta element of spaghetti hoops, like with dry pasta. Other common ingredients include tomatoes (of course), as well as sugar, water, and various spices, such as garlic, onion, and paprika powder.

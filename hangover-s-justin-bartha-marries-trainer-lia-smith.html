Hangovers Justin Bartha Marries Trainer Lia Smith

 Posted on September 12, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  135 words  |  Aldo Pusey

A member of the Wolf Pack is a bachelor no more.

Hangover star Justin Bartha tied the knot with trainer Lia Smith over the weekend.

PHOTOS: ‘The Hangover Part III’: Justin Bartha‘s Instagrams From Set 

The 35-year-old actor married Smith on Saturday in her native Hawaii, on the island of Oahu, according to People. The couple, who became engaged in May, reportedly met at an Equinox in Los Angeles.

Among those in attendance, according to People, were Reese Witherspoon and her husband, Jim Toth, who is Bartha’s agent; Jesse Eisenberg; and Bartha’s Hangover co-star Ken Jeong.

This is the first marriage for Bartha, who also starred on NBC’s short-lived comedy The New Normal and played punk rocker Stiv Bators in the movie CBGB.

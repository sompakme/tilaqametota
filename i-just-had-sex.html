I Just Had Sex

 Posted on August 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  273 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Give I Just Had Sex a spin - it's a new offering from The Lonely Island, featuring Akon. It was dropped on Sunday, December 19th, 2010. The Lonely Island's rap artistry continues to improve with every release, something which is most definitely evidenced on I Just Had Sex. His ever-expanding catalogue is definitely better for it, and we're looking forward to the next piece of the puzzle - aren't you?

Have you heard The Lonely Island's Motherlover (Feat. Justin Timberlake)? If you're down, make sure to visit The Lonely Island's profile page for the newest info, news, songs, and mixtapes. Follow The Lonely Island on Twitter, if you aren't already.

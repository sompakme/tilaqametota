Rick Fox Net Worth 2023: What Is The Actor And Former NBA Player Worth?

 Posted on September 18, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  595 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Rick Fox, a name that resonates with both sports and entertainment enthusiasts, has an estimated net worth of $20 million as of 2023, according to Celebrity Net Worth. This Canadian-Bahamian actor and former professional basketball player has amassed his wealth through a successful career in the NBA, acting, and even a stint in eSports. Fox's journey to his impressive net worth began on the basketball court. His NBA career, which spanned from 1991 to 2004, saw him playing for renowned teams like the Boston Celtics and the Los Angeles Lakers. During his time in the NBA, Fox earned approximately $34 million in salary alone, with his highest single-season salary being $4.8 million from the Boston Celtics.

His basketball prowess was evident from his early years. Born in Toronto, Ontario, Canada, on July 24, 1969, Fox was raised in an athletic family. His mother, Dianne Gerace, was an Olympic high jumper and pentathlete. Fox's basketball journey began in high school and continued at the University of North Carolina, leading the team to the NCAA Final Four in 1991.

From The Court To The Screen: A Successful Transition

Fox's career didn't end with his retirement from the NBA. He transitioned smoothly into acting, a field significantly contributing to his net worth. During his basketball career, he had already appeared in films like Blue Chips, Eddie, and He Got Game. Upon retirement, Fox had more time to focus on acting. He has since appeared in numerous TV series, including One Tree Hill, Ugly Betty, and The Big Bang Theory, among others. His film work post-basketball includes roles in movies like Sharknado 3: Oh Hell No! Fox's presence in reality television, with appearances in shows like Dancing With The Stars and RuPaul's Drag Race, further solidified his place in the entertainment industry.

An Unexpected Venture: eSports

Rick Fox during 2007 Park City - Philips Lounge at Village at the Lift - Day 6 at Philips Lounge in Park City, Utah, United States. (Photo by Mychal Watts/WireImage for Manning, Selvage and Lee)

Fox's wealth accumulation didn't stop at acting. In 2015, he made an unexpected move into the eSports world by acquiring the League of Legends team, Gravity Gaming, which he later rebranded as Echo Fox. Although the venture ended in controversy, it added another layer to Fox's diverse career portfolio. Like many celebrities, Fox has also invested in real estate. In 2013, he listed his luxury apartment in Los Angeles for $1.399 million. Shortly after his retirement from basketball, he had initially purchased the 1,800-square-foot property in 2004 for $1.15 million.

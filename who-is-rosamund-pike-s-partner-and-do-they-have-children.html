Who is Rosamund Pike's partner and do they have children?

 Posted on September 16, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  414 words  |  Martina Birk

ROSAMUND Pike has been the talk of the town since thriller I Care a Lot dropped on Amazon Prime Video.

The Emmy award winning actress is in a long-term relationship with Robie Uniacke. Here's what you need to know...

Who is Rosamund Pike's partner Robie Uniacke?

Robie Uniacke, born in England in 1961, is a mathematician and a businessman.

 Eton-educated Robie is 17 years wife Rosamund's senior.

Back in 2018, the Daily Mail reported that Uniacke had been banned from being a company director for four years after he admitted a number of offences following an investigation into the collapse of his company Pale Fire. 

Robie was previously married to another English actress, Emma Howard, with whom he has a son.

Before that, he was married to interior designer Rose Uniacke.

How did Rosamund Pike and Robie Uniacke meet?

Rosamund, 42, began dating Robie Uniacke in 2009. 

The two reportedly met at a soiree, but they have kept other details private.

Rosamund said about Robie: "I have a very clever partner who's got a very astute mind and is very, very well read and articulate and ruthless about how something I do might play out on screen. 

"My tendency is to identify with a character and imagine there is more on the page than there is. He's quite good at putting a check on that."

Pike told the Guardian that Robie is a feminist.

She added: “It’s a thing I prize highly and appreciate daily. Someone who’s self-confident enough and secure enough to deal with this crazy… It is crazy, how our life is.

"Just the constant shifting. The fact we keep any form of routine is quite remarkable, really.”

Do they have children?

Yes, the couple have two children together.

Their son Solo is eight years old and their son Atom is six.

The family live in a Georgian townhouse in Islington.

Who has Rosamund Pike dated?

Rosamund was previously engaged to the director Joe Wright, after they met while making Pride and Prejudice together.

The Daily Mail reported that their nuptials were cancelled after Rosamund sent out hundreds of wedding invitations without his approval.

The actress later said: "I don't think you ever get over something like that."

Halle Bailey's Sister Ski Apologizes After Calling Out DDG

 Posted on August 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  424 words  |  Martina Birk

A tweet by DDG set the internet on fire after people believed he was shading Halle Bailey. The couple has been going strong for some time, often sharing bits of their romance with fans. However, yesterday (February 7), DDG tweeted, "All these girls the same," causing a firestorm of reactions. People quickly attributed the message to The Little Mermaid star, and rumors of a breakup soon followed. The controversy elicited a reaction from Ski Bailey, Halle's older sister.

In a video, Ski seemed perturbed by seeing Halle's name as a hot topic. Then, she directly addressed DDG and let him know her sister isn't to be messed with, especially publicly. "Halle's not like the ones that you been with," Ski said in her video. "You will not treat her the way that you've treated these others. I don't give a freak if y'all had issues going on. That's what you won't do. Please understand that."

"Like, you're not gonna do her the way that you did everybody else," Ski continued. "You're not gonna come to social media and try to put her on blast and insinuate or put something out that make it seem like she was doing something that she wasn't doing. You need to pipe down and get yourself together. If y'all broke up then you need to just unfollow and just keep it pushin'. You don't have to put nothin' out there like that. Talkin' about, 'All these girls is the same.' Are you dumb? Are you stupid?"

After her clip circulated online, Ski returned with an update. It seemed that she spoke with Halle and got the inside information as to what was going on behind the scenes. "My bad y'all. I spoke to sis and ain't nothing going on lol," she wrote. "Let me go back to drinking my water and minding the business that pays me."

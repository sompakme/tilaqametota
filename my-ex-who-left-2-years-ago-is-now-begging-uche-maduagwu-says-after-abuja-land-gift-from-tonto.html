My Ex Who Left 2 Years Ago Is Now Begging: Uche Maduagwu Says After Abuja Land Gift From Tonto

 Posted on September 23, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  413 words  |  Larita Shotwell
  • Nollywood actor Uche Maduagwu has surfaced on the radar of a former lover again shortly after he became a landed property owner in Abuja
  • In an Instagram post, the actor said the lady has been calling and begging for him to pick things up from where they left it
  • Maduagwu, however, said he’s not sure of her true intentions, and his followers also shared their opinions with him

Nollywood actor Uche Maduagwu has taken to social media with a post announcing that his former lover wants to return to his life.

According to the controversial star, the lady who left two years ago contacted him again after hearing of Tonto Dikeh’s Abuja land gift to him.

The actor wrote:

“Guys, I'm in shock right now, in fact, I'm speechless, my Ex Girlfriend who left me 2 years ago, suddenly started calling and begging to come back after she heard Tontolet gave me a Plot of Land in Abuja."

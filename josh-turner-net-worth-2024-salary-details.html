Who is Josh Turner?

Josh Turner, renowned for his distinctive deep voice, is a celebrated figure in the country music genre. Born on November 20, 1977, in Hannah, South Carolina, Turner has captivated audiences with his traditional country style and gospel influences.

His rise to fame is marked by a commitment to the roots of country music, combined with a modern sensibility that resonates with a wide audience. Known for hits like “Your Man” and “Long Black Train,” Turner’s music is characterized by its depth, both in sound and in storytelling, making him a standout artist in the contemporary country music scene.

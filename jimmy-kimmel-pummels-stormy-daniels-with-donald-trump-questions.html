Jimmy Kimmel Pummels Stormy Daniels With Donald Trump Questions

 Posted on August 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  283 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Last night, Donald Trump gave his State of the Union address to the American people; concurrently, adult film star Stormy Daniels was invited to the Jimmy Kimmel Show for an impromptu and unorthodox interview. 

Daniels, aside from her work in adult entertainment, is most notorious for revealing that she had once had an affair with the POTUS in 2006. However, TMZ reported that they had obtained a letter signed by Daniels which admits that her revelation of a tryst with Trump was false and untruthful. 

In a whirlwind of deceit and slander, Daniels appeared on Kimmel's program last night to "address" the widespread accusations that have been fuelling the rumour mill for a while now. 

First, Kimmel asked Daniels whether or not she had actually signed the newly-released document that expressly details how her affair with Trump was fabricated. Many have compared a previous signature with this recent one, and have pointed out that they appear to be completely different. Daniels responded with "I don’t know. Did I? That doesn’t look like my signature," continuing on to note how "I do not know where that came from. It came from the internet."

However, Daniels' lawyer reveals that she 100% did sign the new document, regardless of how dissimilar the signatures appear to be. 

Daniels then goes on to explain how she is "not denying the affair because I was paid ‘hush money’ as has been reported in overseas owned tabloids. I am denying this affair because it never happened."

This did not stop Kimmel from further indulging in gags about Trump's penis, and presenting a doll to Daniels so she could pinpoint exactly where the president had touched her exactly.
